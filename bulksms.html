Client can send Unlimited SMS per day(Based on the package purchased) in Bulk to mobile users anywhere in India & Abroad using SMSIdea’s Bulk SMS service Platform, No need to purchase any software.

Types Of Bulk SMS Service

Promotional Bulk SMS Service

"Promotional message” means message containing promotional material or advertisement of a product or service.
SMS Will not get delivered on DND Numbers
Sender id is not available for promotional SMS service

Transactional Bulk SMS Service

“Transactional message” means an SMS containing only—
information sent to its customer(s) by the Bank or financial institution or insurance company or credit card company or Access Provider pertaining to the account of that customer(s);any other message as may be specified by the Authority, from time to time as “Transactional message”
SMS will be delivered on DND numbers
6 Character Alphabetic sender id(In Capital Letters) will be provided

International Bulk SMS Service

In International SMS service the SMS can be delivered on International Mobile handsets but different countries have different rules and regulations which affect the delivery styles, like in some countries sender id is not allowed and in some countries DND (Do not Disturb) facility is there which does not allow us the delivery of promotional SMS on DND registered numbers.
